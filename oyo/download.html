Get OYO

Download for your platform. OYO runs as a lightweight background process — always available from your menu bar or system tray.

macOS

Intel & Apple Silicon

Download for Mac

macOS 12 Monterey or later. Installer package (.pkg)

macOS Sequoia+ users

macOS may block the installer because it isn't notarized yet. To install, open Terminal and run:

xattr -cr ~/Downloads/oyo-installer.pkg

Then double-click the .pkg to install normally. We're adding notarization soon so this step won't be needed.

System Requirements

What you need

Hardware

  • 8 GB RAM minimum (16 GB recommended)
  • 2 GB disk space for OYO + browser profiles
  • Internet connection for AI provider API calls
  • Microphone for voice features (optional)

AI Provider (Bring Your Own)

  • OpenAI API key, or
  • Anthropic API key, or
  • Ollama installed locally (fully offline)
  • Keys stored in your OS secure keychain

Voice & Dictation

  • Local speech recognition (faster-whisper)
  • Multiple TTS engines (Kokoro, Edge, System)
  • Custom voice cloning on Apple Silicon
  • Push-to-talk global hotkey

Browser Automation

  • Chromium included (separate from your browser)
  • Persistent profiles with logged-in sessions
  • Works with any web service you can log into
  • Session keepalive runs automatically
Quick Start

Get started in 5 minutes

OYO walks you through everything with a guided onboarding conversation.

1

Install OYO

Run the installer for your platform. OYO adds itself to your menu bar (macOS) or system tray (Windows) and starts automatically at login.

2

Add your AI provider

Enter your OpenAI or Anthropic API key — or point OYO at your local Ollama instance for fully offline operation. Keys are stored in your OS keychain.

3

Connect your accounts

Log into Outlook, Gmail, Slack, or Teams through OYO's browser. Your sessions are persistent — log in once and they stay active.

4

Point to your files

Select folders for OYO to index — OneDrive, Google Drive, project directories. Everything is processed locally and searchable instantly.

5

Start delegating

OYO scans your accounts, builds context, and delivers your first briefing. Review drafts, approve actions, and let OYO handle the follow-through.

Important

Bring your own key.

OYO requires an AI provider to function. You supply your own OpenAI or Anthropic API key, or install Ollama for fully local, offline operation. OYO does not include or resell AI models.
