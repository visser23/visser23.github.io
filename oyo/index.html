What it does

Your work, done.
While you were in that meeting.

OYO doesn't wait to be asked. It's working in the background while you can't be — watching what needs attention, preparing what needs sending, and running deep Projects that produce real deliverables.

Works while you can't

While you're in back-to-back meetings, OYO is watching your inbox. It spots what's gone quiet, drafts the replies you haven't had time to write, and has everything ready for your sign-off when you're free. The work gets done. You just approve it.

Transcribes every meeting — no announcement needed

OYO listens silently to every call. No need to tell the room, no need to ask the organiser for notes afterwards. When the meeting ends, your transcript and action items are already waiting — stored privately on your computer, nowhere else.

Types for you, in any app

Stop typing. Just talk. OYO transcribes your voice into text in whatever you're working in — emails, Slack, Word, Teams. It picks up your tone, learns how you write, and gets faster the more you use it. Other tools charge a subscription just for this.

Knows your world without being told

OYO reads your emails, calendar, and files when you first set it up. Ask it anything — what was agreed in last Thursday's call, what's in that contract, who the key contact is on the Manchester project — and it already knows.

Generates real Office files locally

Need a proper deliverable, not just chat text? OYO can generate Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and PDF files directly on your machine. Files are saved into your local OYO outputs folder and opened with one click.

Projects for bigger pieces of work

For complex asks, OYO can spin up a Project that works in multiple sessions, shows a live activity feed, and delivers a full bundle of outputs (for example strategy docs, research summaries, and slide decks) in one place for review.

Daily briefings so nothing slips

Start the day with a clear briefing: what changed overnight, what is waiting on you, what is blocked, and what can be delegated. OYO turns scattered updates into a single priority list so you can act fast instead of hunting for context.

You control exactly how much it does

Dial it anywhere from "just tell me what needs doing" right up to "handle the routine stuff yourself". You can hand it more responsibility as your confidence grows — and pull it back at any point, instantly.

Completely private — no cloud, no risk

Everything stays on your computer. Your emails, your files, your meetings. No uploading documents to a chatbot. No third-party service sitting on your most sensitive work. Just you and your assistant.

How it works

From "just downloaded" to
genuinely useful — same day.

Setup is a conversation, not a manual. OYO asks you what it needs to know and gets to work.

1

Just talk — it sets itself up

Open OYO and start talking. Tell it your name, your role, how you like to work. You can even give your assistant a name if you like. No forms, no menus, no config files. It's ready in minutes.

2

It learns your world fast

Connect your email, calendar, and files. OYO reads your recent threads, understands your contacts and projects, and gets up to speed on what's already in flight. Within the first session, it knows your world.

3

It starts doing actual work

From day one, OYO is writing emails you haven't had time to send, pulling action items from your meetings, flagging what's gone too quiet, drafting real .docx/.xlsx/.pptx outputs, and running Projects for bigger deliverables. You review and approve. Nothing goes out without you.

4

You end the day ahead

The follow-ups happened. The meeting notes were done before you left the call. The draft you needed was ready and waiting. That's what it feels like to have an assistant who's actually working — not just answering when asked.
