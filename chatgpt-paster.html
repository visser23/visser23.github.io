How it works:

  • Automatically processes content as you type or paste
  • Removes ChatGPT tracking parameters (?utm_source=chatgpt.com)
  • Finds single anchor tags and converts text to "(via text)"
  • Groups consecutive anchor tags as "(via link1, link2)"
  • Works entirely locally in your browser - no data is sent anywhere
