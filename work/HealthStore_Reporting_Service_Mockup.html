What the highest-converting deployments do differently

Clinician‑initiated referral activates 2.3× self‑referral. Deployments where a clinician starts the referral, or where the app is opened during an appointment, reach 48–51% activation. Yours run heavily on self‑referral and app‑only invitations, holding your blended rate at 38%.

What this means for waiting lists

Freed capacity directly reduces backlog pressure. Three-quarters of the 135 million outpatient appointments relate to long-term conditions patients largely manage themselves. Each avoided follow-up, each PIFU conversion, and each prevented admission frees capacity that shortens waits elsewhere.

COPD alone accounts for over 700,000 emergency department attendances and 140,000 emergency admissions annually. The SDE estimate suggests DTx at 10% population coverage could avoid 9,600 of those attendances and 2,100 admissions per year — roughly the emergency COPD workload of 3 large acute trusts.

Limitations and governance boundary

Study limitations

  • Observational design; residual confounding possible despite propensity-score matching on 14 covariates
  • Coding lag in the latest two months of HES data may understate events
  • Exposure defined as first qualifying use; non-adherers diluted the estimate toward null (conservative bias)
  • Single supplier, single condition — not directly generalisable to other products or pathways
  • GP data from GPES; some practices have incomplete submissions
  • No patient-reported outcome measures (PROMs) available in the SDE; self-reported symptom and quality-of-life data remain with the supplier

Governance boundary

SDE evidence is separate from routine reporting. Routine HealthStore data answers “is it being adopted and used?”. SDE evidence answers “did it change real outcomes?”. The two must not be conflated in a business case. Cost-effectiveness figures above use the SDE effect estimate with a 50% confidence adjustment to reflect this uncertainty.

SDE access is governed by the NHS England Secure Data Environment governance framework. Study protocols require independent review. No patient-level data leaves the environment. Only disclosure-checked, suppressed aggregate outputs are published in this report.

SDE study status: not started. CalmPath · NICE EVA conditional positive · Evidence from 2 RCTs showing PHQ-9 reduction of −4.8 pts vs waitlist. HealthStore is not competing with Talking Therapies (IAPT); it covers the specialist and out-of-scope conditions Talking Therapies was not designed for — including severe anxiety with comorbidity, eating disorders, and waiting-list bridging.

The gap HealthStore fills. For conditions outside Talking Therapies scope — specialist SMI technologies (AVATAR Therapy, SlowMo, gameChangeVR), insomnia, eating disorders — there is no national assured pathway today. Each local service rebuilds DTAC, IG, clinical safety, procurement and equity reporting independently. HealthStore is the infrastructure layer for that wider landscape.
SDE study status: planned Q3 2026. HeartStep · NICE EVA positive · 3 RCTs show 15–22% improvement in 6-minute walk test and 28% higher programme completion vs centre-based rehab. Currently only 9 live deployments nationally; SDE cohort building will begin once active user base exceeds 2,000.

Why digital cardiac rehab is the biggest growth opportunity. Two-thirds of eligible patients never start cardiac rehab, primarily due to transport, work and caring barriers. Digital programmes remove those barriers. The heatmap on the Internal tab shows cardiac coverage is under 20% in most regions — the largest coverage gap of any pathway.
